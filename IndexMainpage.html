About KBS

About Us


The KBS AUTOMOBILE is an integrated Automobile Workshop in Musafah M-11, Abu Dhabi, providing Preventive, Corrective and Predictive Maintenance services for light, medium and heavy vehicles. It started off as a small workshop in 2020 and has now grown into an organization through hard work and dedication.

KBS provides quality service in Automobile Maintenance that is widely recognized in the market. The Internal Quality Assurance guidelines are maintained with minimum deviations thus making rarws one of the established workshops in the region.

KBS comprises of a specialized team of Automobile Engineers, Mechanics, Denters, Spray Painters, A/C Mechanics & Auto Electricians that are competent and well trained. Our technical personnel have had multi dimensional “Hands-on” experience in the maintenance of a wide variety of vehicles, petrol or diesel, which include and are not limited to American, European, Japanese, and Korean.
